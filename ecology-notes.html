Ecology Notes

Ideas inspired by nature, systems, and organic movement.

1. Natural Systems

Nature operates through cycles, flows, and feedback loops. Many of my projects borrow these ideas to create movement, structure, and behavior.

2. Atmosphere & Motion

Wind, water, and light all move in ways that feel alive. Simulating these motions helps create digital environments that feel organic.

3. Minimalism in Nature

Nature is complex, but its rules are simple. I try to reflect that in my code — simple rules, emergent behavior.

4. Future Notes

More ecology‑inspired concepts will be added soon — including particle systems, environmental simulations, and movement studies.
